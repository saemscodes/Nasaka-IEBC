What happened

Nasaka IEBC registered over 1.5 million requests this March - 1.4m of which happened in 48 hours. Our infrastructure was not built to handle that kind of overnight growth. We had already started an upgrade when things tipped over. Thus, needing us to either forfeit the site for the next 30 days or to push the upgrade through. That is the honest story. The code side is sorted. What we need now is the budget to push the upgrade through and get the site back up before the 30th - when the CVR push begins.
